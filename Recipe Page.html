Pork Adobo


Pork Adobo is one of the most iconic dishes from
the Philippines increasingly gaining attention worldwide.
Some may even say that this is arguably the Philippines
national dishes, competing alongside other Filipino staples
such as Sinigang, Pancit, or lumpia.

Recipe

        > 1kg og Pork
        > 8 Tablespoons of soy sauce
        > 2 Tablespoons of Vinegar
        > Pepper
        > Oil
        > 5 Cloves of Garlic
        > Onion
        > 2 Bay Leavs
        > Water, just enough to cover the pork
        > 2 Tablespoons of brown sugar
